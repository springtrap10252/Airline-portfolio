Frequently asked

Answers to common travel questions

How early should I arrive at the airport?

We recommend arriving at least 2 hours before departure for domestic travel and 3 hours for international flights.

What is included in my fare?

Most fares include carry-on luggage, in-flight entertainment, and meal service depending on cabin class.

Can I change my booking online?

Yes. Use the Manage Booking panel above to request changes and add extras to your itinerary.
